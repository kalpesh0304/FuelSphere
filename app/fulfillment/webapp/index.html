Fulfillment Workflow — Trigger Chain

A
Fuel Order Received
From Planning App (Airline / Buyer Side)
Airline fuel planner creates a fuel order. It flows to the supplier as a delivery request with flight, station, quantity, and delivery window.
B
Delivery Crew Captures ePOD
Delivery Crew (On-site, mobile/tablet)
Crew selects the fuel order, records meter readings (start/end), temperature, density. Uploads delivery evidence photos and captures pilot signature on device.
C
Fuel Ticket Auto-Created
System (automatic on signature)
Once pilot signs, system automatically generates a fuel ticket under the fuel order with all ePOD data, quantity, density, and timestamps.
D
SAP Document Flow Triggered
System (automatic on ticket creation)
Fuel ticket triggers SAP: Sales Order → Delivery Note → Billing Document (Invoice). All subsequent SAP documents are auto-created via S/4HANA integration.

Fuel Orders — Assigned for Delivery

Select a fuel order to capture ePOD data: meter readings, temperature, density, photos, and pilot signature. On signature, ticket & SAP docs are auto-created.
